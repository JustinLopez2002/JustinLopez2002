Art & Graphic Design

Explore some of the graphic design projects I've worked on.

Meteors Brand Board

This is a brand board for a hypothetical music magazine called 'Meteors'. This was created as part of a graphic design component from the Digital Pipeline [4] Youth Program.

Palm Tree Heart

I painted this in FireAlpaca to celebrate a tropical wedding. I used a limited color palette of orange, purple, pink, and white to create a romantic sunset atmosphere.

Sunflower

This digital watercolor piece emphasizes warm colors and thick outlines to evoke a sunrise-like atmosphere suitable for display or product design.

Mountain

This piece explores cool color palettes and visual contrast without outlines, based on a high school painting assignment that inspired a focus on simplicity and elegance.

Sunflower Card

This is actually a remake of a previous piece. I wanted to experiment with textures and effects in Adobe Illustrator, so I combined the clumped grain effect, and the splatter effect, to create this painted look. However, the splatter effect ended up leaving white spots along the edges of the shape, so I ended up taking the parts of this image into Photoshop to be rasterized, edited, and put back together.
