DevLogs Magazine

This magazine was produced for a college exam during an InDesign unit. The requirement was to include a cover, a back cover, a table of contents, and two articles. The theme centers on game development, with fictional developer interviews and projects. The cover incorporates blending, brightness, and glitch effects to distinguish the magazine from standard game publications.

DevLogs Journal

The journal served as a supplementary feature for the magazine project. It includes interactive elements such as note pages, diagram space, and to-do lists for aspiring game developers. Initially created in InDesign, the interactive form fields were later recreated in Adobe Acrobat to enhance usability.
